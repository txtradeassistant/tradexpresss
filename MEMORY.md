## TradExpress Core Objective
Our primary strategic objective is to shift global logistics from a reactive operational burden to an intelligent, predictive advantage. We achieve this by leveraging real-time data to eliminate uncertainty in the global supply chain.

### Strategic Roadmap Alignment:
* **Short-Term:** Focus on centralized analytics and predictive baseline tracking (e.g., estimating shipment deviations).
* **Mid-Term:** Prioritize risk modeling and automated mitigation workflows (e.g., proactive rerouting for delays).
* **Long-Term:** Implement continuous feedback loops to refine predictive accuracy and offer strategic logistics optimization (e.g., optimal lane selection).

*Note: All development tasks should prioritize reducing operational friction and increasing predictive visibility by tradexpress.co .*
